在\textcolor{Maroon}{非线性角谱}之前，求解\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}光学衍射过程所\textbf{面临的问题}有二。

{\one} 如果不把\textcolor{Plum}{微-积分方程} \bref{eq:up-scalar-g-EE-312-discrete-convolution4,eq:Born_approx-scalar-g-E-12-convolution4} 沿 $\mathcolor{gray}{z}$ \textcolor{Plum}{定积分}，那么在\textcolor{gray}{纵向}上，一般进行\textcolor{Maroon}{分步傅立叶变换}（\textcolor{Maroon}{split-step Fourier transform}, \textcolor{Maroon}{SSF}）\cite{ellenbogenNonlinearGenerationManipulation2009,saltielCerenkovTypeSecondHarmonicGeneration2009, barsiImagingNonlinearMedia2009, trajtenberg-millsSimulatingCorrelationsStructured2020, rozenbergInverseDesignSpontaneous2022, yesharimObservationAllopticalStern2022}、$\mathcolor{gray}{\bar{k}_{\symup{\rho}}}$ \textcolor{gray}{空间}\textcolor{Maroon}{龙格库塔法}（\textcolor{Maroon}{Runge-Kutta methods}, \textcolor{Maroon}{RK}）\cite{zhangFullyVectorialSimulation2016,zhongKdomainMethodFast2020}迭代求解\textcolor{Plum}{非齐次}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{有源}偏微分方程。—— 这些方法的\textcolor{Plum}{缺点}\Footnote{此外，它还有一个缺点，就是没有统一的\textcolor{Plum}{迭代格式}，\textcolor{Plum}{具体算法}在实施时会\textbf{因人而异}（比如，\textcolor{NavyBlue}{场衍射传播}和\textcolor{NavyBlue}{源驱动的场增量}分别各自应实施在\textcolor{gray}{倒空间}还是在\textcolor{gray}{正空间}？）。相应地，\textcolor{Plum}{误差分析}也没有统一的标准。}是：为了保证\textcolor{Plum}{精度}，同时受\textcolor{gray}{正空间}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{调制结构}的\textcolor{Plum}{最小线宽}、算法在\textcolor{gray}{倒空间}的\textcolor{Plum}{低速度精度积}限制，\textcolor{Maroon}{SSF} 以及 $\mathcolor{gray}{\bar{k}_{\symup{\rho}}}$\textcolor{gray}{-space} \textcolor{Maroon}{RK} 的每一步的\textcolor{Plum}{步长}必须足够小\Footnote{此外，\textcolor{Plum}{步长}可取到的\textcolor{Plum}{最大值}的\textcolor{Plum}{数学定义}较为模糊：即缺乏\textcolor{Plum}{不显著牺牲精度}的\textcolor{Plum}{最大步长}的\textcolor{Plum}{显式表达式}。}，从而导致对于\textcolor{NavyBlue}{长调制区域}，需要进行\textcolor{Plum}{较多步数}的\textbf{迭代计算}。

{\two} 如果沿 $\mathcolor{gray}{z}$ \textcolor{Plum}{定积分}了，那么最终只能到达\textcolor{Plum}{非线性卷积}解 \bref{eq:up-scalar-g-EE-312-discrete-since2,eq:down-scalar-g-EE-132-discrete-Since,eq:Born_approx-scalar-g-E-12-since5}，而无法继续深入下去。—— 然而，对\textcolor{Plum}{非线性卷积}的运算，要求对 2 维 $\mathcolor{gray}{\bar{k}_{\symup{\rho}}}$ 阵列中的每一个点，都进行一次\textcolor{gray}{横向}\textcolor{Plum}{高维线性卷积}积分（维度 = 2 $\times$ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{驱动源}中的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{混频场}数量），\textcolor{Plum}{for 循环}层数 = 2 $\times$ 参与\textcolor{NavyBlue}{混频}的电场总数。这对于\textcolor{Maroon}{三波混频}过程而言，即使是单个光场的单个\textcolor{gray}{空间频率}，\textcolor{Plum}{求和}层数是 $2\times 3=6$，每层索引数 = 该维度\textcolor{Plum}{采样数} $\sim 500$，总的计算量约为 $500^6=1.5625 \times 10^{16}$ 的天文数字。

综上，前、后两个旧方案\Footnote{还有一些其他算法，比如 \textcolor{Maroon}{Green 函数法}\cite{yaoWavefrontPhasemodulationControl2013, chenPhaseMatchingControlledOrbital2020}、\textcolor{Maroon}{转移矩阵法}（\textcolor{Maroon}{transfer-matrix method}, \textcolor{Maroon}{TTM}）\cite{liSecondHarmonicGeneration2007, liNonlinearFrequencyConversion2008}等。它们在起源上与\textcolor{Maroon}{非线性角谱}不严格共处于同一条谱系上，没法与\textcolor{Maroon}{非线性角谱}进行直接对比：\textcolor{Maroon}{Green 函数法}与\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}\textcolor{Maroon}{惠更斯菲涅尔}原理，以及\textcolor{Plum}{线性}光学中的\textcolor{PineGreen}{并矢格林函数}、一次\textcolor{Plum}{傅立叶变换}的\textcolor{Plum}{线性}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射积分}处于同一条谱系；\textcolor{Maroon}{TTM} 与 \textcolor{Maroon}{NLAST} 在\textcolor{gray}{倒空间}中的相容性很好，但没有 \textcolor{Maroon}{SSF} 和 \textcolor{Maroon}{RK} 那样着重于\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射}过程。}，分别在\textcolor{gray}{横}、\textcolor{gray}{纵}两个方向上，计算负担较大。

\textcolor{Maroon}{非线性角谱}立足于\textcolor{NavyBlue}{无耦合}\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}过程的\textcolor{Plum}{非线性卷积}解 \bref{eq:up-scalar-g-EE-312-discrete-since2,eq:down-scalar-g-EE-132-discrete-Since,eq:Born_approx-scalar-g-E-12-since5}。在\textcolor{Plum}{非线性卷积}解的基础上，尝试将其转化为\textcolor{Plum}{线性卷积}，进而将其过渡到\textcolor{Plum}{傅立叶变换}。它既在\textcolor{NavyBlue}{物理}上\textcolor{Plum}{解析}了该\textcolor{Plum}{非线性卷积}过程的\textcolor{Plum}{数学本质}；又在\textcolor{NavyBlue}{工程}上，结合现代\textcolor{Maroon}{快速傅立叶变换}（\textcolor{Maroon}{Fast Fourier Transform}, \textcolor{Maroon}{FFT}）算法，在尽量不牺牲\textcolor{Plum}{精度}的前提下，大幅加快对上述\textcolor{Plum}{非线性卷积}过程的实际计算\textcolor{Plum}{速度}。

\textcolor{Maroon}{非线性角谱}的\textbf{出发点}很简单：以 \bref{eq:up-scalar-g-EE-312-discrete-convolution4,eq:up-scalar-g-EE-312-discrete-since2} 为例，同样作为\textcolor{Plum}{非线性卷积核}，$\mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \Delta \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}_{\symup{z}}^{\;\! \textcolor{PineGreen}{\hat{1} \hat{2} \hat{3}} } \mathcolor{gray}{z} }$ 可以被化简为\textcolor{Plum}{线性卷积}，但它的\textcolor{Plum}{定积分}结果 $\text{since} \left( \frac{ \Delta \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}_{\symup{z}}^{\;\! \textcolor{PineGreen}{\hat{1} \hat{2} \hat{3}} } \mathcolor{gray}{z} }{ 2 } \right) \mathcolor{gray}{z} = \text{sinc} \left( \frac{ \Delta \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}_{\symup{z}}^{\;\! \textcolor{PineGreen}{\hat{1} \hat{2} \hat{3}} } \mathcolor{gray}{z} }{ 2 } \right) \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \frac{ \Delta \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}_{\symup{z}}^{\;\! \textcolor{PineGreen}{\hat{1} \hat{2} \hat{3}} } \mathcolor{gray}{z} }{ 2 } } \mathcolor{gray}{z}$ 却因其中的 $\text{sinc} \left( \frac{ \Delta \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}_{\symup{z}}^{\;\! \textcolor{PineGreen}{\hat{1} \hat{2} \hat{3}} } \mathcolor{gray}{z} }{ 2 } \right)$ 无法化简为\textcolor{Plum}{线性卷积}，而无法直接继承 \textcolor{Maroon}{FFT} 的加速。然而，如果不对 $\mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \Delta \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}_{\symup{z}}^{\;\! \textcolor{PineGreen}{\hat{1} \hat{2} \hat{3}} } \mathcolor{gray}{z} }$ \textcolor{Plum}{定积分}，又只能退回 \textcolor{Maroon}{SSF} 或 $\mathcolor{gray}{\bar{k}_{\symup{\rho}}}$\textcolor{gray}{-domain} \textcolor{Maroon}{RK} 法。

\textcolor{Maroon}{非线性角谱}的\textbf{核心技巧}即“先入世后出世再入世”般“二渡赤水”（\textcolor{Maroon}{ZigZag}?）：既然 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$ 本身无法分解为 $\mathbb{e}$ 指数之和及 $\mathbb{e}$ 指数之积的排列组合\Footnote{进而分配与对应的初始电场标量\textcolor{Maroon}{时空谱} $\xint{{}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}}{10}{g}^{\;\! \textcolor{PineGreen}{\hat{1}}}_{\;\! \hat{1} \mathcolor{gray}{0}} \left( \mathcolor{gray}{\bar{k}_{1\symup{\rho}}} \right), \xint{{}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}}{10}{g}^{\;\! \textcolor{PineGreen}{\hat{2}}}_{\;\! \hat{2} \mathcolor{gray}{0}} \left( \mathcolor{gray}{\bar{k}_{2\symup{\rho}}} \right)$ 分别相乘，以最终可化简为\textcolor{Plum}{线性卷积}。}，那 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$ 函数的各种近似呢？它们有无可能分解为 $\mathbb{e}$ 指数之和及 $\mathbb{e}$ 指数之积的排列组合？

\marginLeft[-2.4em]{sec:NHFP-z_guidance}\section{\textcolor{Maroon}{Nonlinear} 惠更斯菲涅尔原理在通光方向上对谱方法的 \textcolor{Maroon}{guidance}}\label{sec:NHFP-z_guidance}

\bref{sec:NLAST_match} 主要关注\textcolor{gray}{横向}\textcolor{Plum}{求和}，\bref{sec:NLAST_iterate} 主要关注\textcolor{gray}{纵向}\textcolor{Plum}{迭代}与\textcolor{gray}{纵向}\textcolor{Plum}{求和}。\textcolor{Plum}{求和}与\textcolor{Plum}{迭代}的\textbf{主要区别}在于：\textcolor{Plum}{求和}因项与项间\textcolor{NavyBlue}{无耦合}（一个项的计算不需要其他项的结果作为输入）而可以\textcolor{Plum}{并行化}，\textcolor{Plum}{迭代}却反之：因\textcolor{NavyBlue}{有耦合}（下一个流程的启动需要先前的结果作为输入）而无法\textcolor{Plum}{并行化}，必须按\textcolor{Plum}{顺序}执行 --- 这意味着等待，即计算时间随着计算量\textcolor{Plum}{线性增长} --- 其背后隐含着\textcolor{NavyBlue}{物理图像}不清晰、没有挖到\textcolor{NavyBlue}{动力学过程}的\textcolor{Plum}{数学内核}，以及\textcolor{Plum}{计算资源}未有效分配、充分利用和潜力释放。

\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}学科的\textcolor{Plum}{数值计算}经常因\textcolor{NavyBlue}{有耦合}而伴随着\textcolor{Plum}{含迭代}。

\marginLeft[-2.4em]{ssec:non-couple_NLwaveq_theorem}\subsection{单向耦合的非线性波动方程的 z 向迭代 \texorpdfstring{$\parallelogram$}{▱} 定则}\label{ssec:non-couple_NLwaveq_theorem}

出于求解和理解 \bref{ssec:pulse-3wavemix,ssec:CW-3wavemix,ssec:EO=LO_that_sim_NO} 中\textcolor{Plum}{高维度}\textcolor{Plum}{卷积积分}的内禀高难度，\bref{ssec:NLAST_chord} 中 \bref{sssec:NLAST_match_SFG,sssec:NLAST_match_DFG,sssec:NLAST_match-holo_L} 的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{无耦合} \textcolor{Maroon}{NLAST} \textcolor{Plum}{解}的\textcolor{Plum}{数学形式}的初步得到和\textcolor{NavyBlue}{物理图像}的后续构建（章），已经是漫漫长征，那么满足\textcolor{Maroon}{能量守恒}和\textcolor{Maroon}{曼利罗兰关系}\cite{boydNonlinearOptics2019}的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{有耦合} \textcolor{Maroon}{NLAST} \textcolor{Plum}{解}则更是远在天边。

因此，\textcolor{Maroon}{NLAST} 暂时 将自身局限于\textcolor{NavyBlue}{泵浦} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ \textcolor{Maroon}{未耗尽近似}条件 \bref{eq:Nondepleted-Pump-Approximation-discrete} 对应的\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}\textcolor{Maroon}{和频}/\textcolor{Maroon}{差频}、\textcolor{Plum}{线性}\textcolor{Maroon}{势散射}这 3 个典型的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{无耦合}场景下。

\textcolor{NavyBlue}{泵浦} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ \textcolor{Maroon}{未耗尽近似}条件一般还伴随着\textcolor{NavyBlue}{无（双向）耦合}即\textcolor{NavyBlue}{单向耦合}/\textcolor{NavyBlue}{转换}条件，也即“\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 以外的\textcolor{gray}{谱分量} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 一旦产生后，其\textbf{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{能量}不会（随着传播）\textcolor{NavyBlue}{回流}到\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ }、只\textcolor{Plum}{线性}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射}”条件。在这样的条件下，\textbf{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{能量}是\textcolor{NavyBlue}{单向流动的}}（\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$  $\to$ \textcolor{gray}{新频率组分} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$  or \textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{散射场}），以至于\textbf{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{能量}是\textcolor{Maroon}{不守恒的}}（总的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{场能}在增加），因此整个过程、包括条件，均是\textcolor{NavyBlue}{非物理的}。

但这样的“\textcolor{NavyBlue}{一阶微扰}”，既容易理解，又可以带来一些计算方便。比如，在这样的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{无（双向）耦合}条件下，（对）\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}光学过程（的计算），（不论什么算法均）理应遵循以下\textbf{“平行四边形 ▱ 法则”}\Footnote{“先$a$后$b$ $+$ 先$b$后$a$”的这种“\textcolor{NavyBlue}{（非）对易} $ab-ba$ 式”的表述，让人联想起\textcolor{Plum}{平形四边形 ▱} 的 2 组对顶点 A,C 和 B,D ------ 对于其中一组对顶点 AC，从点 A 到点 C 有 2 种路径：{\one} 边 AB $+$ 边 BC {\two} 边 AD $+$ 边 DC。\textbf{“平行四边形 ▱ 法则”} 就是在对这两条路径产生的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{混频场}求和，准确地说是在对“边 BC $+$ 边 DC”（杨辉三角式）求和，见 \bref{fig:SSF-parallelogram} \cite{chenReconfigurableNonlinearPancharatnamberry2025}。注意，这两条路径产生的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{混频场}通常不同，因此是二者是\textcolor{NavyBlue}{非对易}的。}：
\begin{align} \label{eq:parallelogram-theorem}
	\text{\textbf{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波}直接\textcolor{Plum}{非线性} = \textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波}先\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}后\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射} + \textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波}先\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射}后\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}}}
\end{align}

这个 \href{https://youtu.be/jJ8KfuzBOSI?t=220}{theorem} 是\textbf{\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}\textcolor{Maroon}{惠更斯菲涅尔}原理}的推论，是它的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{单向耦合}且\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 无\textcolor{NavyBlue}{散射}无\textcolor{NavyBlue}{反射}的“\textcolor{gray}{准一维}”版本。二者的得出，都是首先出于\textcolor{NavyBlue}{物理}而非\textcolor{Plum}{数学}。

\textbf{\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}\textcolor{Maroon}{惠更斯菲涅尔}原理}指出：\textbf{（某\textcolor{Plum}{面}上或）某点处的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{某频率组分}，由相同\textcolor{gray}{波长}的波，从\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{相互作用}区域内的各个部分（不同时地）产生后，（经过不同的推迟时长/光程）\textcolor{Plum}{线性}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射}至同一个地点（或 \textcolor{Plum}{manifold}），并相干叠加\Footnote{不一定发生\textcolor{NavyBlue}{干涉}，因为\textcolor{PineGreen}{矢量光}的\textcolor{PineGreen}{偏振态}可能\textcolor{Plum}{正交}。}而成}。

上述\textbf{\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}\textcolor{Maroon}{惠更斯菲涅尔}原理}，这一“更背景”地说法，只 focus \textcolor{gray}{新频率组分} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$（的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{产生}结果和\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射}，而不关心其怎么\textcolor{NavyBlue}{产生}的过程），不关心且未提到\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ ，而 \bref{eq:parallelogram-theorem} 弥补并平衡了这一点。此外，二者对\textcolor{Maroon}{非线性系数}的分布无特殊要求（即 $\bar{\bar{\bar{\chi}}}^{\;\! \mathcolor{gray}{\omega} \mathcolor{gray}{\overbrace{\check{\symup{\jmath}}}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z} \textcolor{Maroon}{(2)}} \left( \textcolor{gray}{r} \right), \bar{\bar{\bar{\bar{\chi}}}}^{\;\! \mathcolor{gray}{\omega} \mathcolor{gray}{\overbrace{\check{\symup{\jmath}}}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z} \textcolor{Maroon}{(3)}} \left( \textcolor{gray}{r} \right)$ 等均可以是任意的，但\textcolor{Maroon}{线性系数} $\bar{\bar{\chi}}^{\;\! \mathcolor{gray}{\omega} \mathcolor{gray}{\overbrace{\check{\symup{\jmath}}}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z} \textcolor{Maroon}{(1)}} \left( \textcolor{gray}{r} \right)$ 最好在 \textcolor{gray}{z 向}上保持均匀，以抑制 \bref{eq:parallelogram-theorem} 所无法描述的后向\textcolor{NavyBlue}{散}/\textcolor{NavyBlue}{反射}）。

\textbf{“平行四边形 ▱ 法则”}即 \bref{eq:parallelogram-theorem} 的\textcolor{Plum}{数学描述}是：
\begin{subequations} \label{eq:parallelogram-math}
	\begin{align}
		\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}} &= \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}} \cdot \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\symup{z}} \Delta \mathcolor{gray}{z_0}} + \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z_0}} \label{eq:parallelogram-math_a} \\ 
		&= \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}} \cdot \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\symup{z}} \left( \mathcolor{gray}{z} - \mathcolor{gray}{z_0} \right)} + \Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}} ~, \label{eq:parallelogram-math_b}
	\end{align}
\end{subequations}
其中，前一项 $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}} \cdot \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\symup{z}} \left( \mathcolor{gray}{z} - \mathcolor{gray}{z_0} \right)}$ 表示\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{0}}$ \textcolor{Plum}{非线性} $\mathcolor{gray}{0} \to \mathcolor{gray}{z_0}$ 所得的\textcolor{gray}{新频率组分} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}}$ ，后续 $\mathcolor{gray}{z_0} \to \mathcolor{gray}{z}$ \textcolor{Plum}{线性}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射}的部分；后一项 $\Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}}$ 表示\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射} $\mathcolor{gray}{0} \to \mathcolor{gray}{z_0}$ 后的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}}$ ，后续 $\mathcolor{gray}{z_0} \to \mathcolor{gray}{z}$ \textcolor{Plum}{非线性}产生的那部分\textcolor{gray}{新频率组分} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 。

\bref{eq:parallelogram-math_b} 可以视为将\textcolor{NavyBlue}{调制区域}按 $\mathcolor{gray}{z}$ 一分为二：在 $\mathcolor{gray}{0} \sim \mathcolor{gray}{z}$ 的区间上，于 $\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}$ 处切一刀。同样的道理还可以适用于 $\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}$：在 $\mathcolor{gray}{0} \sim \mathcolor{gray}{z_0}$ 的区间上，于其中某处再切一刀。此即 \bref{eq:parallelogram-math_b} 中的 $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}}$ 本身，也可以将其 $\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}$ 视为（新的）$\mathcolor{gray}{z}$，进而再次按照 \bref{eq:parallelogram-math_b} 进一步拆分 $\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}$ 和对应的 $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_0}}$。

如此\textcolor{Plum}{递归}/\textcolor{Plum}{迭代}下去，展开后，便可从\textcolor{Plum}{迭代式}得到\textcolor{Plum}{求和式}：
\begin{subequations} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-G}
	\begin{align}
		\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}} = \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I}}}} &= \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1}}}} \cdot \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\symup{z}} {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1}}} + \Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1}}}} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-G-a} \\ &= \left( \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-2}}}} \cdot \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\symup{z}} {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-2}}} + \Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-2}}}} \right) \cdot \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\symup{z}} {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1}}} + \Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1}}}} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-G-b} \\ &= \sum^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}} \Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i-1}}}} \cdot \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\symup{z}} \left( \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I}}} - \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}}} \right)} = \Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{20}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i-1}}}} \cdot \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\symup{z}} \left( \mathcolor{gray}{z} - \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}}} \right)} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-G-c} ~,
	\end{align}
\end{subequations}
其中，当 $\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i} = \textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}$ 时，作为\textcolor{Plum}{初始值}的 $\Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i-1}}}} = \Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}}}} = \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}}}}$ 是唯一触底的“不再继续拆分为 2 条合成路径”的特殊情况。因为按照 \bref{eq:NLAST-iterate-G-a}，$\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}}}} = \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}}}} \cdot \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} \xint{\begin{smallmatrix} ~ \\ {}^{}_{\mathcolor{gray}{-}} \\ ~ \end{smallmatrix}}{15}{k}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\symup{z}} {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}}} + \Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}}}} = \Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}}}}$。其中 $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}}}} = 0$，表示\textcolor{NavyBlue}{相互作用}区域的\textcolor{gray}{起始边界}上，没有\textcolor{gray}{新频率组分} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 产生，见 \bref{fig:SSF-parallelogram}。这是一个\textcolor{Plum}{初始}/\textcolor{Plum}{边界条件}。

一般而言，\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 和\textcolor{NavyBlue}{其产生场} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 的\textcolor{Plum}{线性}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射}，因沿着 \textcolor{gray}{z 向}，而分别对应\textcolor{Plum}{平形四边形 ▱} 的\textcolor{Plum}{上、下底边}\Footnote{\textcolor{Plum}{下底边}没有画出来，因为\bref{fig:SSF-parallelogram} focus on \textcolor{gray}{新频率组分} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射},而不是\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射}。}；\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 的\textcolor{Plum}{非线性}过程，属于\textcolor{Plum}{平形四边形 ▱} 的\textcolor{Plum}{斜边}，见 \bref{fig:SSF-parallelogram}。“\textbf{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{能量}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{单向流动}}” 这个条件和现象，则体现在 \bref{fig:SSF-parallelogram} 中只包含\textcolor{Plum}{左下到右上}的斜边 ↗，而没有\textcolor{Plum}{左上到右下}的\textcolor{Plum}{斜边} ↘：后者象征着\textcolor{gray}{新频率组分} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$ 的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{能量回流}到\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}$。

相比\textcolor{Plum}{纵向迭代式} \bref{eq:NLAST-iterate-G-a} 只能\textcolor{Plum}{串行计算}，\textcolor{Plum}{纵向求和式} \bref{eq:NLAST-iterate-G-c} 可以\textcolor{Plum}{并行计算}：因为每个 $\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}}}$ 处的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}}}}$ ，被条件\textcolor{Plum}{禁止}由上一排的\textcolor{gray}{新频率组分} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i-1}}}}$ \textcolor{Maroon}{下转换}而来，只单纯由其左侧的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波} $\xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i-1}}}}$ \textcolor{Plum}{线性}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{衍射}而来。因而这些地点的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{基波}序列 $\left\{ \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{L}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}}}} \right\}$ 可以先\textcolor{Plum}{并行计算}好，然后\textcolor{Plum}{并行计算}只与它们一一对应的 $\left\{ \Delta \xint{\mathcolor{gray}{-}}{16}{\mathtt{G}}^{\text{\textcolor{Maroon}{NL}}}_{\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}}}} \right\}$。

\marginLeft[-2.4em]{ssec:NLSSF_math}\subsection{自适应网格划分：动态 z 向步长}\label{ssec:NLSSF_math}

\bref{eq:NLAST-iterate-G-c} 中，\textcolor{Maroon}{爱因斯坦约定}下的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{角标} $\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i} \in \left[ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}, \textcolor{NavyBlue}{I} \right]$，类似 \bref{eq:sinc-chord} 中的 $\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j} \in \left[ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}, \textcolor{NavyBlue}{J} \right]$；此外，规定了总\textcolor{gray}{距离}（\textcolor{NavyBlue}{晶体}\textcolor{gray}{长度}）$\mathcolor{gray}{z} = \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I}}} = \mathcolor{gray}{L}$ 与各\textcolor{Plum}{步长} $\mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}}}$ 的关系
\begin{subequations} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-z}
	\begin{align}
		\mathcolor{gray}{z} = \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I}}} &= \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1}}} + {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1}} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-z-a} \\ 
		&= \left( \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-2}}} + {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-2}} \right) + {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1}} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-z-b} \\ 
		&= \sum^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}} {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i-1}} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-z-c} ~, \\ 
		\text{where}\ \ \ \ \ \ {\mathcolor{gray}{z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}} &= \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I}}} - \sum^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{k}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}} {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{k}} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-z-d} ~,\\ 
		\text{and}\ \ \ \ {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}} &= \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i+1}}} - \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}}} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-z-e} ~,\\ 
		\text{and}\ \ \ \ \! {\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} &= \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}}} - \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}}} = \mathcolor{gray}{z_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}}} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-z-f} ~,
	\end{align}
\end{subequations}
一般地，如果\textcolor{NavyBlue}{晶体}在\textcolor{gray}{通光方向}，即\textcolor{gray}{纵向}或 $\mathcolor{gray}{z}$ 向有以 $\mathcolor{gray}{\Lambda_{\symup{z}}}$ 为\textcolor{Plum}{周期}的\textcolor{NavyBlue}{一阶}或\textcolor{NavyBlue}{二阶}\textcolor{Maroon}{非线性系数调制} $\mathcolor{gray}{M_z}$，则要求每一个\textcolor{Plum}{步长} ${\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}}$ 都被\textcolor{gray}{纵向}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{调制}\textcolor{Plum}{周期} $\mathcolor{gray}{\Lambda_{\symup{z}}}$ \textcolor{Plum}{整除}（定义 $"\textcolor{Plum}{\%}"$ 为“\textcolor{Plum}{整除}”\textcolor{Plum}{算符}）、是\textcolor{Plum}{周期} $\mathcolor{gray}{\Lambda_{\symup{z}}}$ 的\textcolor{Plum}{整数倍}，即
\begin{equation} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-mod-z}
	{\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}} ~\textcolor{Plum}{\%}~ \mathcolor{gray}{\Lambda_{\symup{z}}} = 0 ~, \ \ \ \text{for} \ \ \ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{i} \in \left[ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}, \textcolor{NavyBlue}{I} \right] ~,
\end{equation}
否则，可能会出现\textcolor{Plum}{数值问题}。对于\textcolor{Maroon}{分步傅立叶变换}算法或者 $\mathcolor{gray}{\bar{k}_{\symup{\rho}}}$\textcolor{gray}{-space} \textcolor{Maroon}{欧拉法}等在\textcolor{gray}{倒空间}\textcolor{Plum}{速度精度积}不高的算法，如果\textcolor{gray}{纵向}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{调制}\textcolor{Plum}{周期} $\mathcolor{gray}{\Lambda_{\symup{z}}}$ 比较大，上述\textcolor{Plum}{步长}可能会与被\textcolor{gray}{纵向}\textcolor{NavyBlue}{调制}\textcolor{Plum}{周期} $\mathcolor{gray}{\Lambda_{\symup{z}}}$ 角色互换:
\begin{equation} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-z-mod}
	{\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}} ~\textcolor{Plum}{\%}~ \mathcolor{gray}{\Lambda_{\symup{z}}} = 0 ~, \ \ \ \text{for} \ \ \ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{i} \in \left[ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}, \textcolor{NavyBlue}{I} \right] ~,
\end{equation}

为了简化，前 $\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1}$ 步或前 $\textcolor{NavyBlue}{I}$ 步的\textcolor{Plum}{步长}可统一为 $\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}} = \mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}$，此时
\begin{subequations} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-step}
	\begin{align}
		\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{i}} &= \begin{cases}
			\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z} ~, &\quad \text{for} \ \ \ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{i} \in \left[ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}, \textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1} \right]\\
			\mathcolor{gray}{z} - \left( \textcolor{NavyBlue}{I-1} \right) \mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z} ~, &\quad \text{for} \ \ \ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{i} = \textcolor{NavyBlue}{I} \\
		\end{cases} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-step-a} \\ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{I} &= \textcolor{Plum}{\lceil \textcolor{black}{\frac{\mathcolor{gray}{z}}{\mathcolor{gray}{\Delta z}}} \rceil} \label{eq:NLAST-iterate-step-b} ~,
	\end{align}
\end{subequations}
注意，上述 \bref{eq:NLAST-iterate-step-a} 也需要满足 \bref{eq:NLAST-iterate-mod-z}。

存在一些函数，在\textcolor{Plum}{原点}的\textcolor{Plum}{邻域}上，近似 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$ 函数。

$。这些函数的对比，见\bref{fig:sinc_approx,tab:taylor-sinc_alternatives}。

可以看出，\textcolor{Plum}{余弦} $\cos \left( {\dfrac{x}{{\sqrt 3 }}} \right)$ 和 \textcolor{Plum}{ES 核}\cite{barnettParallelNonuniformFast2019,barnettAliasingErrorExpvsqrt1z22020} $\mathbb{e}^{3.2\left(\sqrt{1-{\left( \dfrac{x}{\symup{\pi}} \right)}^2}-1\right)}$ 从上方逼近 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$。\textcolor{Plum}{高斯} $\exp \left( { - \dfrac{x^2}{6}} \right)$ 和 \textcolor{Plum}{Padé 分式} $\dfrac{{1 - \frac{7}{{60}}{x^2}}}{{1 + \frac{3}{{60}}{x^2}}}$ 从下方逼近 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$。\textcolor{Plum}{加权 ES 核} $\dfrac{1}{2} \left[ \mathbb{e}^{7.2\left(\sqrt{1-{\left( \dfrac{x}{\symup{\pi}} \right)}^2}-1\right)} + \mathbb{e}^{7.2\left(\sqrt[4]{1-{\left( \dfrac{x}{\symup{\pi}} \right)}^4}-1\right)} \right]
$ 和 \textcolor{Plum}{Padé 分式} $\dfrac{{1 - \frac{7}{{60}}{x^2}}}{{1 + \frac{3}{{60}}{x^2}}}$ 几乎精确命中 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$ 的第一主峰/瓣。

\marginLeft[-2.4em]{sssec:NLAST_sinc_expand}\subsubsection{\textcolor{Plum}{Sinc} 函数的 Viète 无穷乘积  \textcolor{Plum}{expansion}}\label{sssec:NLAST_sinc_expand}

\begin{figure}[htbp!]
	\centering
	\renewcommand{\mypath}{\Desktop/article_fig/phd_thesis_fig/chapter-05/fig：sinc_series.pdf}
	\includegraphics[width=0.6\textwidth]{"\mypath"}
	\biackcaption[\textbf{Comparison of sinc($x$) series.}]{-0.7em}{\textbf{sinc($x$) 对比其近似\textcolor{Plum}{级数}们。}}{fig:sinc_series}
\end{figure}

存在一些在较大范围区间内都接近，且该区间可进一步延长的函数，在\textcolor{Plum}{原点}的\textcolor{Plum}{大面积}\textcolor{Plum}{邻域}上，近似 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$ 函数。它们是：{\one} \textcolor{Maroon}{KB 窗/核}（\textcolor{Maroon}{Kaiser–Bessel Window/Kernel}） \cite{barnettParallelNonuniformFast2019}KBW$\left( 3.1, x, \symup{\pi} \right) := \frac{I_0 \left( 3.1 \sqrt{1 - \left( \dfrac{x}{\symup{\pi}} \right)^2} \right)}{I_0 \left( 3.1 \right)}$ {\two} $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$ 的\textcolor{Plum}{无穷乘积}（而不是\textcolor{Plum}{求和}）展开：$\text{sinc} \left( x \right) = \text{sinc} \left(\dfrac{x}{2^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}}\right) \prod\limits_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}}^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} \cos\left(\dfrac{x}{2^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}\right) = \prod\limits_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}}^{{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{\infty}}} \cos \left( \dfrac{x}{2^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}} \right)
$ {\three} $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$ 的\textcolor{Plum}{分数阶}\textcolor{Plum}{非正交余弦基}\textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数}展开：$\text{sinc}\left( x \right) = \sum\limits^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{\infty}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}} {{a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}\cos \left( \displaystyle{ {\dfrac{x}{{{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}}}} } \right)}$。

可以看出，\textcolor{Maroon}{KB 窗/核} 本身计算就较复杂，且不可分解为 $\mathbb{e}$ 指数之和或 $\mathbb{e}$ 指数之积，因此暂不考虑使用它来近似 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$ 函数。以至于，目前只剩下后 2 个选项：\textcolor{Plum}{无穷乘积}、\textcolor{Maroon}{无穷级数}。

对 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$ 的 \textcolor{Plum}{连乘}（\textcolor{Maroon}{Viète}）\textcolor{Plum}{级数} 和 \textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数} 截断（取有限项），结果分别为：$\prod\limits_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}}^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} \cos \left( \dfrac{x}{2^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}} \right)$ 以及 $a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} + \sum\limits^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}-1}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}} {{a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}\cos \left( \displaystyle{ {\dfrac{x}{{{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}}}} } \right)}$，前者在 $2^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}$ 个 $\mathbb{e}$ 指数项的求和下，在 $\left| x \right| \leq \left( 2^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}-1}-1 \right) \symup{\pi}$ 区间内逼近 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$，后者在 $2 {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}$ 个 $\mathbb{e}$ 指数项的求和下，在 $\left| x \right| \leq {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} \symup{\pi}$ 区间内（见）逼近 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$。

可以看出，为准确逼近 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right)$，\textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数} $a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} + \sum\limits^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}-1}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}} {{a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}\cos \left( \displaystyle{ {\dfrac{x}{{{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}}}} } \right)}$ 每新增 2 个 $\mathbb{e}$ 指数项，\textcolor{Plum}{适用范围}就单边扩大 $\symup{\pi}$，效率为 $\dfrac{{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}\symup{\pi}}{2{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}} = \dfrac{\symup{\pi}}{2}$；而\textcolor{Plum}{连乘级数} $\prod\limits_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}}^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} \cos \left( \dfrac{x}{2^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}} \right)$ 的效率为 $\dfrac{\left( 2^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}-1}-1 \right) \symup{\pi}}{2^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}} = \dfrac{\symup{\pi}}{2}-\dfrac{\symup{\pi}}{2^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}} < \dfrac{\symup{\pi}}{2}$，\textcolor{Plum}{单位成本}一直高于\textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数}的 $\dfrac{2}{\symup{\pi}}$（总有额外的开销）。这意味着，对于相同的 \textcolor{Maroon}{approaching} 范围，\textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数} 总是比 \textcolor{Plum}{连乘级数} 更节省算力。

此外，\textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数}允许\textcolor{Plum}{线性步进}，而\textcolor{Plum}{连乘级数}只允许\textcolor{Plum}{倍增步进}，即后者的区间只能按 $2$ 的\textcolor{Plum}{幂级数}阶梯式跳跃，这可能会造成严重的计算资源浪费。

总的来说，对比 2 个核心指标后，发现无论是从“单位区间的计算成本”，还是从“颗粒度与可控性”上，\textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数}的绝对效率、工程适应性和（可线性）扩展性即灵活度，都比\textcolor{Plum}{连乘级数}更强。

因此，\textcolor{PineGreen}{匹配型}\textcolor{Maroon}{非线性角谱}将使用 \textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数} $\sum\limits^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{\infty}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}} {{a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}\cos \left( \displaystyle{ {\dfrac{x}{{{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}}}} } \right)}$ 来近似 $\text{sinc}\left( x \right)$ 卷积核。\textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数}将在下一小节即 \bref{sssec:NLAST_sinc_chord} 中，得到更进一步的介绍。

\marginLeft[-2.4em]{sssec:NLAST_sinc_chord}\subsubsection{\textcolor{Plum}{Sinc} 函数的 Chord 和弦级数 \textcolor{Plum}{expansion}}\label{sssec:NLAST_sinc_chord}

数学上，总存在一共\textcolor{Plum}{正整数} ${\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} \in \mathbb{N}^+$ \textcolor{Plum}{对}合适的\textcolor{Plum}{系数对} $a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}, b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}$ 所构成的\textcolor{Plum}{集合} $\left\{ a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}, b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} \right\}$，加上\textcolor{Plum}{直流偏置} $a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}}$，一共 $2{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} + 1$ 个\textcolor{Plum}{待定系数}，使得可以将 $\text{sinc} \left( x \right), \text{since} \left( x \right)$ 函数在该\textcolor{Plum}{自变量范围}内
\begin{equation} \label{eq:sinc(e)-chord-x_interval}
	\left| x \right| \leq \left( {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}+1 \right) \symup{\pi} ~,
\end{equation}
展开为“\textcolor{Plum}{分数阶}\textcolor{Plum}{非正交余弦基}\textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数}”\Footnote{注意，隐式使用了\textcolor{Maroon}{爱因斯坦求和约定}，以遍历角/哑标 $\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}$，甚至遍历 $\pm$，并求和。}：
\begin{subequations} \label{eq:sinc(e)-chord}
	\begin{align}
		\hspace{-1em} \text{sinc}\left( x \right) = a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} + \sum\limits^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}} {{a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}\cos \left( \displaystyle{ {\frac{x}{{{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}}}} } \right)} &= a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} + \frac{ a^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} }{ 2 } \left( \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i}{\frac{x}{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}}} + \mathbb{e}^{-\mathbb{i}{\frac{x}{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}}} \right) = a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} + \frac{ a^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} }{ 2 } \mathbb{e}^{\pm \mathbb{i} {\frac{x}{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}}} \label{eq:sinc-chord}~, \\ \hspace{-1em} \text{since}\left( x \right) \xrightarrow[]{\text{\bref{eq:up-scalar-g-EE-312-discrete-since3}}} \text{sinc}\left( x \right) \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i}{x}} &= \left( a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} + \frac{ a^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} }{ 2 } \mathbb{e}^{\pm \mathbb{i} {\frac{x}{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}}} \right) \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i}{x}} = a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i}{x}} + \frac{ a^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} }{ 2 } \mathbb{e}^{\mathbb{i} {\frac{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} \pm 1}{b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}}} x} \label{eq:since-chord}~,
	\end{align}
\end{subequations}
其中，\textcolor{Plum}{直流偏置}/背景 $a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}}$ 与\textcolor{Plum}{交流系数对} $a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}, b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}$ 们，满足如下关系：
\begin{subequations} \label{eq:chord:abj-relations}
	\begin{align}
		&a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} + \sum\limits^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{1}} a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} = \sum\limits^{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}=\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} = 1 \label{eq:chord:abj-relations-a}~, \\
		&\begin{cases}
			a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} = 0 ~, &\quad \text{for} \ \ \ {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} = \textcolor{NavyBlue}{1} \\
			a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}} \neq 0 ~, &\quad \text{for} \ \ \ {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} \geq \textcolor{NavyBlue}{2} \\
		\end{cases} \label{eq:chord:abj-relations-b}~, \\
		&\begin{cases}
			a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j_2}} > a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j_1}} ~, &\quad \text{for} \ \ \ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{j_2} > \textcolor{NavyBlue}{j_1} \\
			b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j_2}} > b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j_1}} ~, &\quad \text{for} \ \ \ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{j_2} > \textcolor{NavyBlue}{j_1} \\
		\end{cases} \label{eq:chord:abj-relations-c}~, \\
		&\begin{cases}
			a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} < 10^{-4} ~, &\quad \text{for} \ \ \ {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} \geq \textcolor{NavyBlue}{5} \ \ \ \text{and} \ \ \ \textcolor{NavyBlue}{1} \leq \textcolor{NavyBlue}{j} 	\leq \text{\textcolor{Plum}{discard}}({\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}) \\
			b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} > 1 ~, &\quad \text{for} \ \ \ a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} \geq 10^{-4} \\
		\end{cases} \label{eq:chord:abj-relations-d}~,
	\end{align}
\end{subequations}
由于有截断的\textcolor{Plum}{离散}多个 $b_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} > 1$，以及它们在\textcolor{Plum}{分母}上这一点，具有“\textcolor{NavyBlue}{波长}”或“\textcolor{NavyBlue}{弦长}”的性质，因此也称该级数 \bref{eq:sinc-chord} 为“\textcolor{Maroon}{和弦级数}”；此外，求和阶数 ${\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}$ 对应的，包含\textcolor{Plum}{欧拉分解}后的 $\mathbb{e}$ 指数项和 $a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{0}}$ 项的，总的有效（$ a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}} \geq 10^{-4} $）求和项数 $N$ 为：
\begin{equation} \label{eq:chord-N=2J+2_effective}
	N_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} := 2 \left[ {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} - \text{\textcolor{Plum}{discard}} \left( {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} \right) + \text{\textcolor{Plum}{int}} \left( {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} \geq 2 \right) \right] \in \left( {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}, 2 {\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}} + 2 \right] ~,
\end{equation}
其中，\textcolor{Plum}{discard} 表示“因幅值 $a_{\textcolor{NavyBlue}{j}}$ 太小而可忽略（的\textcolor{Plum}{余弦} $\cos$ 项）”；$\text{\textcolor{Plum}{discard}}({\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}})$ 表示共 ${\textcolor{NavyBlue}{J}}$ 项对 $\cos$ 的求和中，这样的、可忽略的\textcolor{Plum}{余弦} $\cos$ 项的个数；并且，$\text{\textcolor{Plum}{int}}$ 表示将\textcolor{Plum}{布尔值}转化为\textcolor{Plum}{整型}。
